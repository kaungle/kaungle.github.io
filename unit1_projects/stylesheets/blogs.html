Design/UI Reflections

There are many amazing websites out there, from small blogs to sophisticated web apps like Google Maps. Three of my favorites are Earmilk, Soundcloud, and Hype Machine. All music websites, I really appreciate the innovative ways each brings a better music experience to users. Earmilk is a great example of the potential of small blogs. When I am on the homepage, the black background and neon text elements immediately grab my attention. Even when stepping away from the laptop screen a bit, the color palette is just as striking. Images and tables are well formatted, inviting the user to scroll down the page.
